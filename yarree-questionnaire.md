## 🔮 LEVEL 6: The Oracle Round

*This is where it gets fun. These questions help us figure out which career pathways to prioritize. Go with your gut.*

### The Acupuncture Questions
1. What drew you to acupuncture in the first place?
2. What kind of patients or conditions get you most excited?
3. Vibe check — are you more drawn to intimate 1:1 sessions or the community/group model (recliners, sliding scale, multiple patients)?
4. How do you feel about the business side — marketing, billing, insurance paperwork?
5. Would you rather work at someone else's clinic first or jump straight into your own thing?

### The Dog Training Questions
6. How does dog training feel compared to your other work — does it give you energy or take it away?
7. Is there a type of dog or behavior issue you particularly love working with?
8. Would you be into running group classes?
9. Big picture: do you want to grow this into a real business, or keep it as a chill side thing?

### The Anthropology Questions
10. Did you enjoy the research and academic parts of the M.S., or was it more of a grind?
11. What was the most interesting project or piece of research you did?
12. If you could apply those skills somewhere — health, tech, nonprofits, culture, education — where would it be?
13. Any professional connections from PSU you could tap into?

### The Real Talk Questions
14. What's your monthly number — roughly what do you need to bring in to keep the lights on?
15. Can you reduce hours at Crema, or is it all-or-nothing?
16. How important are benefits (health insurance, retirement) vs. flexibility and doing your own thing?
17. Remote work — into it, or do you need to be out in the world?
18. Is there a work environment where you thrive?
19. Is there anything you absolutely, positively DO NOT want to do?

### The Dream Round
20. If money were handled and you could design your days — what would they look like?
21. Does the idea of "building a brand" excite you or make you want to hide?
22. Any interest in teaching, writing, workshops, or public-facing content?
23. On the Crema Escape Velocity Scale™ — where are you?
