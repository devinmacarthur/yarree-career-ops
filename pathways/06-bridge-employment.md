# Pathway 6: Bridge Employment (Better W-2, Non-Food-Service)

## Overview
If Yarree needs to leave Crema before the longer-term pathways are generating income, this is the "get a better job now" track. Not a career destination — a bridge to something bigger.

## Criteria for a Good Bridge Job
- Not food & beverage
- Pays at least as well as Crema (ideally better)
- Less physically/emotionally draining
- Offers some transferable skills or connections toward target career
- Flexible enough to accommodate acupuncture school completion and side business

## Bridge Job Options (Ranked by Strategic Value)

### Tier 1: Directly Supports a Target Pathway

**Wellness Clinic Front Desk / Office Manager**
- Pay: $18–24/hr ($37–50K)
- Why: Learn clinic operations from the inside before launching own practice
- Where: acupuncture clinics, naturopathic offices, chiropractic, massage therapy studios
- Bonus: networking with practitioners, understanding insurance billing, patient flow

**Veterinary Clinic Receptionist / Technician Assistant**
- Pay: $17–22/hr ($35–46K)
- Why: Reinforces animal expertise, builds referral network for dog training
- Where: Portland has many vet clinics; also consider VCA, Banfield, independent practices
- Note: Vet tech requires certification, but receptionist/assistant does not

**Research Assistant / Coordinator (PSU or OHSU)**
- Pay: $20–28/hr ($42–58K)
- Why: Uses anthropology skills, keeps academic connections alive, could lead to Pathway 5 roles
- Where: PSU, OHSU, Reed College, PCC — check university job boards
- Bonus: Often includes tuition benefits if Yarree wants additional training

### Tier 2: Good Pay, Better Quality of Life

**Library Associate / Assistant**
- Pay: $18–24/hr ($37–50K) — Multnomah County Library pays well
- Why: Calm environment, community-facing, values education
- Where: Multnomah County Library system (check governmentjobs.com)
- Bonus: County benefits, PERS retirement, union

**Administrative / Office Coordinator**
- Pay: $20–28/hr ($42–58K)
- Why: Transferable skills, regular hours, often flexible
- Where: Nonprofits, universities, healthcare systems, small businesses
- Good stepping stone to program coordinator or management roles

**Retail Management**
- Pay: $40–55K salaried
- Why: Yarree's customer service skills translate directly; management experience is valuable
- Where: REI, New Seasons, Powell's, local Portland businesses
- Better hours and benefits than cafe work

### Tier 3: Flexible / Gig Options

**Rover / Wag Premium Pet Sitter**
- Pay: Variable, $30–60K possible in Portland if aggressive
- Why: Already doing this work — platform access could increase volume
- Note: Platform takes 15–20%, but provides client pipeline

**Substitute Teaching**
- Pay: $180–220/day in Portland Public Schools
- Why: Flexible schedule, only need a bachelor's degree + sub license
- Where: PPS, Parkrose, David Douglas, Centennial districts

**Executive / Virtual Assistant**
- Pay: $20–30/hr
- Why: Leverages organizational skills, can be remote/flexible
- Where: Belay, Time Etc, or local businesses

## Job Search Resources (Portland-Specific)
- **Mac's List** — Portland's best job board for nonprofits and mid-market
- **Idealist** — nonprofit focus
- **governmentjobs.com** — county, state, city roles
- **PSU job board** — alumni have access
- **Oregon Employment Department** — iMatchSkills
- **Indeed / LinkedIn** — general
- **Craigslist Portland** — still active for local small business jobs

## Resume Approach for Bridge Jobs
- Lead with customer service experience and reliability
- Emphasize organizational and operational skills from running own business
- Mention M.S. only if relevant to the role (some bridge jobs it helps, some it intimidates)
- Keep it to one page
- Highlight: communication, multitasking, client management, self-directed work
