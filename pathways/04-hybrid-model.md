# Pathway 4: Hybrid Model — Acupuncture + Dog Training as Side Income

## Overview
Run acupuncture as the primary career while keeping Dog Heart PDX / Open Hand Pet Care as supplemental income. Based on Yarrow's questionnaire answers, this isn't a 50/50 split — it's acupuncture-forward with dog work filling gaps and providing stability during the ramp-up.

## Why Hybrid (Not Full Dual Business)
Yarrow's answers make clear that acupuncture is the career and dog training is the side hustle:
- Acupuncture: "mostly excited and prepared" about business side
- Dog training: "energizing but still a learning curve, need to find niche"
- Dog business vision: "not sure yet"
- Group classes: "maybe"
- Dog training is already generating income — don't abandon it, but don't try to scale it right now

## The Role of Dog Heart PDX in the Transition
- **Financial bridge** during acupuncture ramp-up
- **Schedule filler** — dog walks/visits/sitting between acupuncture patients
- **Low-stress income** — word-of-mouth clients, established relationships
- **Seasonal complement** — sitting peaks during holidays, acupuncture is steadier
- **No pressure to grow** — just maintain current clients and income
